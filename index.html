Take the Unknown Out of Your Travel


We understand that preparing for international travel can be a scary and overwhelming prospect at times. That's why we at CoddiWomple came together to design an app that consolidates all the key travel information needed for you to feel comfortable, secure, and prepared in over hundreds of countries.

So go ahead, grab your coddiwomplin' shoes, we've got you covered wherever they might lead.

Cultural Awareness

"Why is everyone staring at me?"

It can be difficult navigating new cultures. We have all built unconscious habits over a lifetime in our own cultures, and not all of them translate well. Here at CoddiWomple we have talked with locals from different regions all over the world to build a comprehensive list of common traveling mistakes made when visiting particular countries.

“Travel is fatal to prejudice, bigotry, and narrow-mindedness, and many of our people need it sorely on these accounts. Broad, wholesome, charitable views of men and things cannot be acquired by vegetating in one little corner of the earth all one's lifetime.”

Mark Twain, The Innocents Abroad / Roughing It

Survival Language

"Wait...the turtle did what to who??"

One of the most stressful parts about traveling internationally can be communication. CoddiWomple has the essential who, what, where, why, and when survival phrases for each country entry, allowing you to decrease your stress levels while increasing your local interaction.
